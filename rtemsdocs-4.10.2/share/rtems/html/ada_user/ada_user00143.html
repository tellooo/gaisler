8.3.6: Resetting a Timer

The rtems.timer_reset directive is used to restore an interval timer initiated by a previous invocation of rtems.timer_fire_after or rtems.timer_server_fire_after to its original interval length. If the timer has not been used or the last usage of this timer was by the rtems.timer_fire_when or rtems.timer_server_fire_when directive, then an error is returned. The timer service routine is not changed or fired by this directive.
