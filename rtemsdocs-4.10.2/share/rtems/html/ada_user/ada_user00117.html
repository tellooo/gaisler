7.3.2: Setting the Time

The rtems.clock_set directive allows a task or an ISR to set the date and time maintained by RTEMS. If setting the date and time causes any outstanding timers to pass their deadline, then the expired timers will be fired during the invocation of the rtems.clock_set directive.
