3.10: Copy the ada Subdirectory to the GCC Source Tree

Copy the ada subtree in the patched subtree of tools/gnat-3.13p-src/src to the tools/gcc-2.8.1 directory: 

cd tools/gnat-3.13p-src/src
cp -r ada ../../gcc-2.8.1
